- **Method:** deb payload extracted with `dpkg-deb -x`

## Dependency closure

Real ELF is `/usr/bin/gs-gpl`. `readelf -d` needs: `libpaper.so.1`,
`libXt/SM/ICE/Xext/X11.so.6`, `libpng12.so.0`, `libjpeg.so.62`, `libz.so.1`,
`libm`, `libc`. (Unlike 8.01, no gimpprint dependency.)

All sonames are satisfied from etch (`apt`): `libpaper1 libxt6 libsm6 libice6
libxext6 libx11-6 libpng12-0 libjpeg62 zlib1g`, plus `binutils gdb wget`.

After extraction: `ldconfig` and `ln -sf /usr/bin/gs-gpl /usr/bin/gs`.
